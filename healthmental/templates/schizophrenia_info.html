Gain insights into schizophrenia, its symptoms, causes, and treatment options to support yourself or a loved one.

What is Schizophrenia?

Schizophrenia is a serious mental disorder in which people interpret reality abnormally. It can result in hallucinations, delusions, and disorganized thinking and behavior that impairs daily functioning.

Symptoms

Common symptoms include:

  • Delusions (false beliefs not based in reality)
  • Hallucinations (seeing or hearing things that donâ€™t exist)
  • Disorganized thinking and speech
  • Negative symptoms (reduced emotional expression, lack of motivation)

Causes

The exact cause is unknown but may involve a combination of genetics, brain chemistry, and environmental factors. Risk factors include family history, complications during birth, and substance use.

Treatment

Treatment usually includes a combination of medications (antipsychotics), psychotherapy, social support, and self-care strategies. With proper management, many individuals can lead fulfilling lives.
