About the PHQ-9 Depression Test

The PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire-9) is a scientifically validated depression screening tool used worldwide by healthcare professionals. It helps diagnose the severity of clinical and chronic depression, including symptoms like hopelessness, loss of interest, and emotional fatigue.

Key Symptoms Measured

  • Persistent sadness or emptiness
  • Lack of interest or pleasure in activities
  • Fatigue and low energy
  • Insomnia or oversleeping
  • Appetite or weight changes
  • Feelings of worthlessness or guilt
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Thoughts of death or self-harm

Who Should Take This Test?

Our free depression questionnaire is ideal for anyone experiencing stress, anxiety, or emotional changes. It can help identify postpartum depression in new mothers, major depressive disorder in adults, and stress-anxiety-depression combinations in all age groups.

Types of Depression Assessed

  • Major Depression
  • Chronic Depression (Dysthymia)
  • Postpartum Depression
  • Depression with Anxiety
  • Clinical Depression
  • Multidimensional Depression Spectrum
