Why Take an Anxiety Test Online?

Millions suffer silently from anxiety, depression, and related disorders. Our online anxiety test is a helpful tool for identifying symptoms of social anxiety, health anxiety, and generalized anxiety disorder (GAD) in adults and teens.

Symptoms of Anxiety & Depression

  • Persistent feelings of worry or panic
  • Social withdrawal or fear of social settings
  • Racing thoughts or constant overthinking
  • Tiredness, sleep problems, or muscle tension
  • Difficulty focusing or restlessness
  • Feelings of hopelessness or sadness

Who Are These Tests For?

Our free anxiety tests are suitable for:

  • Teenagers facing social pressure or academic anxiety
  • Anyone suspecting signs of depression or phobia

Test Types Available

Yes, our tests are based on established clinical screening tools like the GAD-7, which is widely used by healthcare professionals worldwide.

How accurate are online anxiety tests?

Online tests provide a good initial screening but cannot replace a professional diagnosis. They help identify potential symptoms that may warrant further evaluation.

Yes, all test results are completely confidential and not stored on our servers. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

Can teenagers take these tests?

Yes, our tests are designed to be appropriate for both adults and teenagers, though parental guidance is recommended for younger users.
