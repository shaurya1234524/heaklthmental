What is the Bipolar Test?

This free Bipolar Disorder Test is based on the MDQ (Mood Disorder Questionnaire), a clinically recognized tool for identifying Bipolar Spectrum Disorders. It helps screen for symptoms of Bipolar I, Bipolar II, and Mixed Episodes.

Common Symptoms of Bipolar Disorder

  • Severe mood swings ranging from mania to depression
  • Unusually elevated or irritable mood (mania or hypomania)
  • Impulsive or risky behaviors (during high phases)
  • Fatigue, sadness, or hopelessness (during depressive phases)
  • Changes in sleep patterns or energy levels
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions

Why Take a Bipolar Assessment?

Many people with Bipolar II Disorder or Cyclothymia go undiagnosed for years. Taking an online bipolar test can be the first step in understanding your mood patterns and getting the right diagnosis and care.

Who Is This Test For?

This bipolar quiz is suitable for:

  • Adults experiencing mood swings or emotional highs and lows
  • Teens showing signs of manic depression or unusual behavior shifts
  • Anyone looking for a preliminary bipolar self-diagnosis

How It Works

The test consists of a series of questions about your mood, behavior, and thought patterns. Your answers help evaluate whether your experiences align with symptoms of Bipolar I or Bipolar II disorder. Please note: this test is not a diagnosis, but a tool to guide you toward further evaluation.
