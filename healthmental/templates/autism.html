Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) Test

Our free Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) test is designed to help identify potential autistic traits based on established clinical screening tools. This self-assessment can provide insights into whether you might benefit from further professional evaluation.

About the ASD Test

The Autism Spectrum Disorder test uses questions based on the Autism Spectrum Quotient (AQ) and other validated clinical tools. It assesses various aspects of autistic traits including:

  • Social interaction patterns - How you engage with others
  • Communication styles - Verbal and non-verbal communication
  • Repetitive behaviors - Patterns of behavior and interests
  • Sensory sensitivities - Responses to sensory input

Who Should Take This Test?

This test is appropriate for:

  • Adults who suspect they may have undiagnosed autism
  • Teens and young adults seeking self-understanding
  • Parents concerned about their child's development
  • Individuals exploring whether they might be on the autism spectrum

Understanding Your Results

After completing the test, you'll receive a score indicating the likelihood of autistic traits. If the score suggests a higher probability of ASD, you may be advised to seek further professional evaluation. If the score is low, it's unlikely that you have ASD, but this test should not replace a formal diagnosis from a healthcare professional.
