What is ADHD?

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a spectrum neurodevelopmental condition that affects memory, focus, and self-control. It impacts people across all age groups, from children and teens to adults and older individuals. Symptoms may vary widely by age and gender.

ADHD Symptoms & Spectrum

Common signs of ADHD include difficulty staying focused, hyperactivity, impulsive actions, and forgetfulness. The spectrum ranges from mild inattentive symptoms to severe hyperactivity and disorganization:

  • Inability to stay organized
  • Frequent memory lapses and distractions
  • Fidgeting and restlessness
  • Difficulty finishing tasks or following instructions
  • Interrupting or talking excessively
  • Feeling overwhelmed in quiet or structured settings
